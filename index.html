Welcome to FLAME Research Lab

FLAME is a hub of innovation and collaboration, committed to advancing the field of Natural Language Processing (NLP). Our team of dedicated researchers, students, and engineers work together to develop cutting-edge algorithms that enhance machines' ability to process, generate, and understand human language. Our research covers a broad spectrum, from foundational work in computational linguistics and machine learning to practical applications in human language technology.

Our lab is not just about research; we actively contribute to the broader NLP community. We create and share educational resources and tools, enabling others to innovate in NLP. At FLAME, we are driven by the transformative potential of language technology and are committed to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in this exciting field.

FLAME is closely aligned with leading AI research initiatives and collaborates with experts across various disciplines to drive the next generation of language models and applications. Join us as we explore the future of language and technology.
