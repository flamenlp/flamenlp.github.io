Welcome to FLAME Research Lab


Frontier in Language Modelling is a hub of innovation and collaboration, committed to advancing the field of Natural Language Processing (NLP). Our team of dedicated researchers, students, and engineers work together to develop cutting-edge algorithms that enhance machines' ability to process, generate, and understand human language. Our research covers a broad spectrum, from foundational work in computational linguistics and machine learning to practical applications in human language technology.
